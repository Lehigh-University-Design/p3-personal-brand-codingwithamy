Moo Deng's Growth on Social Media

September 2, 2024: Moo Deng first time going viral was thanks to a post on X with the caption of "MOO DENG," along with four pictures of the shiny, plump creature looking ready to bite.

Her popularity spiked on September 10, when a photo of Moo Deng biting a leg went viral on X.

The peak of the Moo Deng-mania took place on September 28, when a Saturday Night Live skit portrayed Moo Deng, cosplayed by the comedian Bowen Wang, complaining about the downsides of fame. Although the skit created controversy around potentially mocking Chappell Roan, it created a lot of buzz around the Moo Deng name. That day, Moo Deng was mentioned 205,000 times.

Even brands hopped onto the Moo Deng trend. FOr example, Sephora Thailand published an Instagram post on September 11, featuring makeup products inspired by Moo Deng's rosy pink cheeks. This post kicked off the Moo Deng makeup microtrend where beauty gurus make looks that reflected the innocent and cute look of Moo Deng.

On September 20, the U.S. Department of Labor used a picture of Moo Deng getting sprayed with water to rack up tens of thousands of engagement actions on X and promote hydration at the same time.

Moo Deng hit her highest mentions spike on September 28, when a dog food brand Spot & Tango drove a surge of social engagement with its post.
